Nimble Feather

Computer Vulnerability Assessments & Network Protection

Nimble Feather is a product that conducts non-exploitive scans, and assesses client security vulnerabilities. Non-exploitive scans looks for weaknesses in a system that an attacker could exploit, and offers snapshot reports showing any vulnerabilities as well as the steps needed to mitigate them.
