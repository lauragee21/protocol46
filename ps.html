Products & Services

Nimble Feather

Computer Vulnerability Assessments & Network Protection

Nimble Feather is a product that conducts non-exploitive scans, and assesses client security vulnerabilities. Non-exploitive scans looks for weaknesses in a system that an attacker could exploit, and offers snapshot reports showing any vulnerabilities as well as the steps needed to mitigate them.

Stonewall Aegis

Threat Identification and Mitigation

Stonewall Aegis is an adaptive and intelligent software that provides network and data loss prevention. Aegis is intelligent as it prevents unauthorized data downloads from the client network. Unlike current firewalls Aegis is adaptive, and learns what is normal on the client network, so that when new traffic is detected Aegis can determine if the new traffic is hostile or not.

Titanium Castle

Endpoint Protection

Titanium Castle protects devices that are both on and off the network with Anti-Malware Behavioral Analysis, Network Use Scanner, Content Control, Anti-Phishing, and Firewall protection. Titanium Castle manages and monitors mobile devices including phones, tablets, and computers.
